## 1. Introduction

Welcome to DepositIQ ("the App", "we", "our", or "us"). DepositIQ is a financial calculator application designed to help users calculate DPS (Deposit Pension Scheme), FDR (Fixed Deposit Receipt), EMI (Equated Monthly Installment), loan amortization schedules, and goal-based savings plans.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using DepositIQ, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

DepositIQ is designed with privacy-first principles. We do **not** collect, store, transmit, or share:

- Your name, email address, or contact information
- Any financial data you enter into the calculators
- Your bank account details, savings amounts, or personal finance information
- Location data
- Device contacts, photos, or files
- Login credentials of any kind

**All calculations are performed 100% locally on your device and are never sent to any server.**

## 3. Information Collected Automatically (via Third-Party SDKs)

### 3.1 Google AdMob (Advertising)

DepositIQ displays banner advertisements served by **Google AdMob**, a third-party advertising service by Google LLC.

Google AdMob may automatically collect:
- Device advertising identifiers (Android Advertising ID)
- IP address (used to infer approximate location for ad targeting)
- Device model, OS version, screen size
- App usage and interaction data with ads
- Crash and performance data

This data is collected by Google, not by us directly. Google uses this data to serve relevant advertisements.

**You can opt out of personalized advertising** by:
- Going to **Settings → Privacy → Ads** on your Android device
- Enabling "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 3.2 Expo / React Native Framework

The app is built on Expo and React Native. These frameworks may collect anonymous crash reports and performance metrics to help improve app stability. No personally identifiable information is included.
