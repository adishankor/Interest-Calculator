## FULL DESCRIPTION (4000 characters max)

💎 **DepositIQ — Your All-in-One Smart Finance Calculator**

Are you looking to grow your savings, plan for a dream purchase, or understand your loan repayments? DepositIQ is the most complete and user-friendly financial calculator app designed for everyday people — no finance degree needed!

---

**🏦 DPS CALCULATOR**
Planning a monthly deposit scheme with your bank? DepositIQ calculates your exact maturity amount based on your monthly installment, interest rate, and tenure — with both **Simple** and **Compound** interest options.

---

**💰 FDR CALCULATOR**
Got a lump sum to invest? Calculate your Fixed Deposit Receipt (FDR) maturity value with support for all compounding frequencies — **Annual, Semi-Annual, Quarterly, and Monthly**. Know your exact returns before you invest!

---

**📊 EMI CALCULATOR**
Need a loan? Enter your loan amount, interest rate, and tenure to instantly calculate your **Equated Monthly Installment (EMI)**, total interest payable, and total repayment — so you can borrow confidently.

---

**📅 LOAN AMORTIZATION SCHEDULE**
Go deeper with your loan! View a **month-by-month breakdown** of how each EMI payment is split between principal and interest. Watch your outstanding balance reduce over time and plan your prepayments strategically.

---

**🎯 GOAL PLANNER CALCULATOR**
This is where the magic happens! Tell DepositIQ your dream:
• How much money you want to accumulate
• When you want to achieve it
• Your expected return rate

DepositIQ will tell you **exactly how much to save monthly (SIP)** AND **how much to invest today as a lump sum** to reach your goal — whether it's a house, car, vacation, education, or emergency fund!
